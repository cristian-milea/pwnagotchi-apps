# Privacy Policy — Ink Cartridge

_Last updated: 30 May 2026_

Ink Cartridge ("the app") is a companion app for an e-ink handheld device that
you own. This policy explains what data the app handles. The short version: the
app has no account system, shows no ads, contains no third-party analytics or
tracking SDKs, and **sends no personal data to the developer**.

## Who we are

Ink Cartridge is developed by Cristian Milea. Contact:
**inkcartridge@cristimilea.ro**.

## What the app accesses, and why

- **Your device's status data.** The app connects directly to your own
  device over Bluetooth (and, where applicable, your local network) to display
  status such as battery, uptime, and on-screen content. This data is read from
  your device and stored only on your phone.

- **Approximate location (optional).** If you turn it on in a cartridge's
  settings, the app reads your phone's *approximate* location so cartridges that
  show local information (for example, weather) can work. The location is used
  to look up a place name on your phone and is sent to the third-party data
  service that a given cartridge is configured to use (for example, a public
  weather API) in order to fetch that data. Location is never sent to the
  developer and is never used for advertising or tracking. You can leave it off.

- **Secrets / API keys you enter.** Some cartridges need an API key or token.
  These are stored **encrypted on your phone** (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain)
  and are sent only to the service that the cartridge you configured them for
  talks to. They are never sent to the developer.

- **Cartridge files.** When you install a cartridge from the catalog, the app
  downloads its files from the public catalog source and transfers them to your
  own device. No personal data is involved.

## What we do **not** do

- No user accounts, sign-in, or profiles.
- No advertising and no advertising identifiers.
- No third-party analytics, tracking, or crash-reporting SDKs.
- No selling or sharing of personal data.
- No collection of your data on any server operated by the developer — the
  developer operates no servers for this app.

## Data storage and retention

All data the app holds — device snapshots, settings, and encrypted secrets —
lives on your phone. Removing it is as simple as clearing the app's data or
uninstalling the app.

## Network communication

The app communicates with: (1) your own device, over Bluetooth / your local
network; (2) the public cartridge catalog source, to download cartridges; and
(3) only the third-party data services that the cartridges you choose to use are
configured to call. The app does not send your data anywhere else.

## Children

The app is a technical companion tool and is not directed at children.
